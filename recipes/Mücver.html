Mücver

Mücver is a Turkish fritter or pancake, made from grated zucchini. They are typically pan-fried in oil and their batter often includes a mixture of eggs, onion, dill, parsley, flour, and sometimes potatoes and cheese (beyaz peynir or kaşar). They are similar to Jewish latkes and potato pancakes from various cultures.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium green zucchini
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons of flour
  • 1 handful of chopped parsley
  • An optional handful of spring onions, dill, fresh mint, etc.
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil for frying
  • Garlic yoghurt for serving

Steps

  1. Wash the zucchini and peel it if you deem it necessary (if it is bruised or hard)
  2. Put a strainer in a deep bowl and grate the zucchini into the strainer with the fine side of the grater
  3. Squeeze out the water by pressing the strainer well with your palm
  4. Finally, take it piece by piece with your palm, squeeze it well and put it in a separate container
  5. Crack the eggs onto the zucchini and whisk until well mixed
  6. Add the baking soda, salt and pepper to the flour and mix
  7. Slowly add the flour to the pumpkin-egg mixture, whisking
  8. Add your last chopped greens and mix
  9. Heat a little oil in a wide pan
  10. Take a spoonful of the mixture, put it in the pan and level it with the back of the spoon
  11. Fry the whole mixture, cooking 4 pieces at a time
  12. You can serve it by pouring garlic yogurt on it
